Projector Trace Tool

Turn student photos into projector-friendly tracing silhouettes and outlines.

Steps: Upload → Adjust → Threshold → Project → Trace

Step 1
Upload Student Photo
Start Here
Bright side-lighting works best. Head and shoulders only if possible.
Try Cast Shadow first. If it’s too solid, switch to Bold Outline.
Step 2
Image Adjust
Increase until face details feel strong but not blown out.
Add blur to smooth out busy backgrounds and tiny details.
Step 3
Sensitivity
Slide until features are clear lines without losing the face shape.
If your student is in front of a dark wall or shadow, turn this off. If the wall is bright and the student is darker, leave it off and adjust contrast instead.
Step 4
Projector Setup
Step 5
Export for Tracing
Final Step
Teacher tip: Set your projector to “blackout” or a blank slide, then open this PNG and tap F11 / full screen so the tracing fills the wall or paper.
